About Our Laboratory

The Computer Science Research Laboratory at DHU is dedicated to pioneering research that pushes the boundaries of computing technologies. Established in 2016, our lab brings together exceptional faculty, researchers, and students to tackle complex challenges in Federated learning, distributed machine learning, security and privacy protection, wireless communication networks, edge intelligent computing, stream computing, cloud-edge resource scheduling.

We collaborate with industry partners and other academic institutions to develop practical solutions that address real-world problems. Our team has published in top-tier conferences and journals, and our work has been recognized with multiple awards and grants.
